About Me

As an active and ambitious participant in life, many subjects and experiences fascinate me. I graduated from UC Santa Barbara with my bachelors in chemistry, and have worked in several tech start-ups and VC environments since. The scrappy world of software and technology funding has captivated me and groomed me into finding greater interests in technical roles using new skills and learned assets. In 2017, I joined the UC Berkeley Full Stack Development Bootcamp to do just that. Coupled with an innate thirst for innovation, adventure, and reasonably priced spirits, I came into software because I wanted to explore tools that would allow me to build and contribute towards the rise of new technologies that create positive impact.


I moved back to the Bay Area to train, create, build and inspire. I'm always open to sharing fresh ideas and new perspectives. Feel free to message me any time to discuss any or all of these interests together. I look forward to collaborating with you all very soon!
